Summary:

    Navigation:
        Tab: Go to the next item.
        Shift + Tab: Go to the previous item.

    Documentation Scrolling:
        Control + b: Scroll up through documentation.
        Control + f: Scroll down through documentation.

    Completion Control:
        Control + Space: Manually trigger the completion menu.
        Control + e: Abort the completion.
        Enter: Confirm the selection and insert it into the code.
